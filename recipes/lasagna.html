Homemade Lasagna Recipie

  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1 (28 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 1/2 tespoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/8 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 (16 oiunce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tabllespoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded mozzerella cheese
  1. Step 1

    Combine pork and ground beef in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minues. Add onion and cook until translucen, about 5 minutes.

  2. Step 2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes.
  3. Step 3. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  4. Step 4. While the noodles are cooking, preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
